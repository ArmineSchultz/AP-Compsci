FANTASY _ EASY _ LEVEL 2 CLUES


Offerings to the Great Mountain Dragon

Every year, messengers from different kingdoms
are sent to give gifts to the Great Mountain
Dragon in return for bountiful crops and other
agricultural things. Find out which kingdom and 
gifts Klyde, Elric, Mira and Jade coming bearing 
gifts from.

1. The messenger from Emberfall brought the Golden
   Crown.

2. Kylde is not from Permaforma.

3. The messenger from Stormreach brought the
   Enchanted Sword.

4. Elric is from Dawnmere.

5. Jade did not bring the Jeweled Chalice.

6. The messenger from Permaforma brought the
   Mystic Amulet.

7. Mira is not from Emberfall.

8. Kylde brought the Jeweled Chalice.
