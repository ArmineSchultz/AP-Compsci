CRIME _ MEDIUM _ LEVEL 2 CLUES


An expensive watch gone missing

Three luxurious watches were on display at a
jewelry store. The dark sapphire blue watch 
in the center was stolen by either Kacy, 
Lena, Henry or Claude. Each was found in a
different area, at different times and in 
possession of different objects. 
It is your job to find the thief!

1. The person at the Front Desk was there at
   11:00 AM.

2. Lena was seen at 1:00 PM.

3. The person with the Wallet was in the Back
   Room.

4. Henry was not in the Display Room.

5. The Hallway was used at 2:00 PM.

6. The person with the Bag was seen earlier
   than Claude.

7. Kacy was in the Display Room.

8. The person with the Sunglass was at the 
   Front Desk. 

9. Claude was not in the Back Room.

10. The person with the Receipt was seen at 
    12:00 PM.
